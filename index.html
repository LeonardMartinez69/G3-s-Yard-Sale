About Us

Welcome to G3 Garage Sale! At Adamson University, Computer Science students Miles Baldres, Freidrich Fajardo, Ledmor Martija, Princess Miraflores, and James Radan launched the campus’s first-ever garage sale. What began as a simple idea to declutter their rooms and give new life to unused items quickly grew into a vibrant community event—with a meaningful purpose. Beyond offering budget-friendly finds, G3 Garage Sale is driven by a commitment to support charitable initiatives, turning everyday items into opportunities to help others.

What We Do

At G3 Garage Sale, we collect and sell preloved items at affordable prices to promote sustainable shopping on campus. By giving unused items a new home, we reduce waste while raising funds to support charitable causes—turning simple sales into meaningful impact.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a welcoming garage-sale platform where quality, budget-friendly items are accessible to all while promoting reuse and reducing waste. Through every sale, we aim to extend kindness by donating a portion of our proceeds and resources to charitable initiatives, strengthening our community and making a positive impact on those in need.

Our Vision

To create a community where affordability, sustainability, and compassion come together—turning secondhand goods into meaningful support for families and charitable causes.
